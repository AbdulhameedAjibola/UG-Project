    What is VoteSmart?

    VoteSmart is a secure and transparent platform designed to facilitate online voting for various elections and surveys. Our platform ensures the integrity and confidentiality of each vote, providing a reliable alternative to traditional voting methods.

    Our mission is to empower communities and organizations by offering a user-friendly voting system that simplifies the voting process while maintaining the highest standards of security. We strive to make every vote count and believe in the power of technology to enhance democratic participation. Join us in making voting easier and more accessible for everyone.

    Wondering How to get Started? Follow these Simple Steps:

    Step 1: Register or Log In

    Open the app and create a new account using your email or phone number. If you already have an account, simply log in with your credentials.

    Step 2: Verify Your Identity

    Complete the identity verification process by uploading a photo ID and taking a selfie. This ensures the security and integrity of your vote.

    Step 3: Cast Your Vote

    Once verified, you can view the ballot and cast your vote. Make sure to review your choices before submitting. Your vote is important!
